About the Academy

Introducing Taleem Ul Quran Online Quran Academy, a distinguished institution founded in 2012 by Maulana Umar Qayoom, a renowned Islamic scholar. Initially established as a physical academy with centers in the UAE and Pakistan, we have since transitioned to an online platform to extend our reach and provide high-quality Quranic education worldwide. Committed to excellence, our academy is dedicated to providing high-quality Quranic education exclusively tailored for males, females, and children. Our leadership team, comprised of diverse and passionate women and men, spearheads our mission to illuminate hearts with the profound teachings of the Quran. At our academy, certified female Almiah, Hafizah, and Qariah Quran teachers guide and nurture the learning journey of our students. While we prioritize empowering mothers, sisters, daughters, and children in their pursuit of Quranic knowledge, we also extend our services to boys with parental consent. Through a supportive environment and a commitment to excellence, we endeavor to enrich the spiritual journey of all our students, fostering a deep understanding and appreciation of the Quran.

Our Teaching Methodology

At Taleem Ul Quran Online Quran Academy, we employ cutting-edge online platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and MS Teams to facilitate dynamic and immersive Quranic learning experiences. Our teaching philosophy prioritizes student engagement, fostering an environment where interactive participation is encouraged and valued. We believe in harnessing the power of technology to deliver high-quality education that is accessible to all.
In addition to Zoom and Google Meet, our academy leverages the versatility of MS Teams, ensuring that students can seamlessly access their classes from any electronic device, be it a computer, smartphone, or tablet. With features like screen sharing, we empower our students to participate in their lessons from any location, granting them the flexibility to tailor their learning experience to their individual needs. At Taleem Ul Quran, we are committed to providing a modern and convenient approach to Quranic education, enabling our students to embark on their spiritual journey with ease and confidence."
